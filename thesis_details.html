This research was a part of my Master's Thesis, advised by Dr. PK (IIIT Delhi) , Dr. Dubey (Accenture Technology Labs Bangalore).

The project aims to study the process of crowdsourcing thoroughly, identify points from where confidentiality leaks could occur, the types of vulnerabilities, and suggest solutions to ensure confidentiality of data before it is posted on a crowdsourcing platform by an organization.
